# Recovery Protocols

## Introduction
This document outlines the recovery protocols for our systems.

## Sovereign Protocols
1. **Backup Systems**: Ensure all critical data is backed up regularly.
2. **Incident Response**: Follow the incident response plan when a failure occurs.

## Cockpit-Grade Protocols
1. **User Communication**: Inform all users of any expected downtime.
2. **System Check**: Perform a health check on all relevant systems after recovery.
